# ZXG Wellness SEO Content Workflow

Use this workflow to publish 2–3 high-quality articles each week without letting AI output go live unedited.

## Weekly Topics

- Creatine: performance routines, consistency, hydration, recovery support, beginner education.
- Recovery: post-workout rituals, skin care, rest, body balm routines, wellness habits.
- Accessories: reusable pens, cartridges, pen needles, product comparisons, setup explainers.
- Customer education: shipping, returns, order tracking, how-to guides, product selection.

## Somnus AI Draft Prompt

```text
Write a helpful, brand-safe SEO article for ZXG Wellness.

Topic:
[INSERT TOPIC]

Primary keyword:
[INSERT KEYWORD]

Audience:
Customers shopping for premium wellness products who need clear, non-medical product education.

Requirements:
- Use a premium but simple brand voice.
- Do not make medical claims, treatment claims, or dosing promises.
- Aim for 2,000–3,500 words for core authority articles.
- Include a clear introduction, quick answer, table of contents, 4–8 sections, 3–6 FAQs, and internal link suggestions.
- Mention relevant ZXG products naturally where useful.
- Include references from trusted sources such as PubMed, NIH, FDA, MedlinePlus, or professional organizations when making health, supplement, safety, or science-adjacent claims.
- Add an educational disclaimer when discussing supplements or accessories.
- Keep paragraphs short and easy to read on mobile.
```

## Human Edit Checklist

- Confirm the article is accurate and does not give personal medical advice.
- Confirm the author profile, reviewed date, updated date, and references are present.
- Add internal links to relevant product, blog, how-to, contact, and return pages.
- Check that the title and meta description match the search intent.
- Add FAQs that answer real buying questions.
- Add concise direct-answer paragraphs before longer explanations for AI search visibility.
- Keep claims conservative: use “supports,” “helps build a routine,” and “designed for.”
- Use comparison tables, bullets, definitions, and entity-rich headings where helpful.
- Publish only after proofreading for grammar, brand tone, and readability.

## Authority Page Structure

- Quick answer: directly answer the main search question in 2–4 sentences.
- Context: define important terms before deeper explanations.
- Evidence: summarize what trusted references say without overstating claims.
- Practical guidance: explain how readers can apply the information safely.
- Internal links: connect to related articles, products, how-to pages, and support pages.
- FAQ: answer specific customer/search questions in plain language.
- References: list source title, organization/journal, and URL.

## Publishing Rhythm

- Monday: Creatine or wellness education article.
- Wednesday: Recovery or skincare article.
- Friday: Accessories, buying guide, or comparison article.
